The Birth of Pixar

Pixar Animation Studios

Steve Jobs purchases the Computer
Division from George Lucas and establishes the group as an
independent company, “Pixar.” At this time about 40 people are employed
Pixar and Disney begin collaboration on CAPS,
the Computer Animation Production System,
which would revolutionize the creation of traditional animated films
The first check written to Pixar by a client after its incorporation
is from Disney, for work on this project
